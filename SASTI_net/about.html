Monitoring abnatural activity for a safer world

SASTI, fully known as the Supernatural and Advanced Science and Technology Investigation program, is an organization that established its independence from Metal Project in 1995.

Its mission is to keep Earth safe from both humans utilizing power outside of human understanding and those with technological ingenuity far surpassing those of orginary humans, who pose a threat to our safety and security as a whole. Research has shown such 'abnatural' cases to perform an extraordinary rate of violent activities, fulfilling the phrase, “power tends to corrupt.” As a result, we have formed the ultimate defense program to combat abnatural menaces and to keep the public safe and unaware.
